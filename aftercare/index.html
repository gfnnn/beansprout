Aftercare

Second Skin

Cling film

  1. When you are ready to apply aftercare, gently remove the cling film as soon as possible.
  2. Wash your hands thoroughly with antibacterial soap.
  3. Lather a non-scented soap in your hands and gently clean the tattoo to remove plasma and debris.
  4. Rinse with lukewarm (not hot) water and pat dry with a clean paper towel.
  5. Let the tattoo air dry for 20 minutes, then apply a thin layer of tattoo aftercare moisturiser (we recommend Palmer’s Cocoa Butter).
  6. Repeat this process twice daily for 2–4 weeks
  7. Continue moisturising regularly after healing to maintain vibrancy.

  2. Wash your hands thoroughly with antibacterial soap.
  3. Gently peel off the bandage under running water, pulling upward and away from the tattoo.
  4. Lather a non-scented soap in your hands and gently clean the tattoo to remove plasma and debris.
  5. Rinse with lukewarm (not hot) water and pat dry with a clean paper towel.
  6. Let the tattoo air dry for 20 minutes, then apply a thin layer of tattoo aftercare moisturiser (we recommend Palmer’s Cocoa Butter).
  7. Repeat this process twice daily for 2–3 weeks
  8. Continue moisturising regularly after healing to maintain vibrancy.
